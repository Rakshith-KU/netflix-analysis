## Overview
Analysis of Netflix movies and TV shows over 20 years to uncover trends in content types, genres, release years, and country production.

## Tools
- **Python**: Data analysis and manipulation
- **Pandas**: Data cleaning and wrangling
- **NumPy**: Numerical analysis
- **Matplotlib & Seaborn**: Visualization and storytelling

## Key Insights
- Netflix shows are growing faster than movies in recent years.
- US dominates content production; India and UK follow.
- Top genres: Drama, Comedy, International, Action.
